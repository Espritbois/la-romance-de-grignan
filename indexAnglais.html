La Romance de Grignan

Located in Grignan, 12 km from Drôme Provençale Golf Course, La Romance de Grignan offers recently renovated accommodation with free WiFi and a terrace.
Valdaine Golf Course is about 21 km away, while Crocodiles Farm is 23 km away and 27 km from the International Candy Museum.
This non-smoking property is 19 km from University of Wine.
This air-conditioned holiday home features 2 bedrooms, a living room, a fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher and coffee machine, and 2 bathrooms with a bath and hairdryer. Towels and bed linen are provided. There is also a seating area and a fireplace.
The property is 36 km from Orange Golf Course and 39 km from Orange Ancient Theatre. Avignon-Provence Airport is 75 km away.
